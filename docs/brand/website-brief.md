## About

Island Stampede is Tasmania's only professional indoor bull riding event, bringing together Australia's best riders alongside international competitors from Brazil and New Zealand.

It is more than a sporting event — it is a premium entertainment experience featuring elite bull riding, live music, crowd entertainment, big-screen production, and a high-energy atmosphere.

This is a not-for-profit event supporting the Launceston Children's Ward Auxiliary.
